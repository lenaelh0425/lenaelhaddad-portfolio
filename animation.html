City of Ottawa Transportation

After a full 8 months, I am glad to present my final capstone project with the City of Ottawa's transportation department. I was given the task to turn the City of Ottawa's data asset management report on transportation into something more fun and easier to digest for the general public.
Everything you see was illustrated, animated, and edited by a couple of other teammates and me. We decided on pixel art because we felt it was a professional way to portray a cartoon animation while keeping the audience engaged. We also went with a quite colourful palette to ensure the audience does not get bored by dull saturation.
Role Animator, Narrator, Graphic Designer Client City of Ottawa: Transportation Tools Pixilart.com, Premiere Pro Year 2025-2026
