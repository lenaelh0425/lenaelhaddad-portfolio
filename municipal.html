Website Design
Ottawa Municipality System

This website was a team effort! In our Civics and Engagement class, we were to teach about the City of Ottawa and its municipal system through a website. My team and I focused on design and consistency, using our colour inspiration based on the City of Ottawa logo.
My participation in this website apart from communication and planning, was solely creating the "City Hall" page, and contributing in the "Homepage", "Municipal Issues", and "Ward".
