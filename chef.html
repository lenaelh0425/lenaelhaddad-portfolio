Videography/Video Editing
Chef Ric Watson

I created a short documentary alongside two teammates. We wanted to focus on food insecurity in Ottawa, and we had the opportunity to interview Ric Watson. We recieved an A+ on this assignment, and was asked if it could be shown at an Enrichment Program on the foundations of visual communication.
Chef Ric Watson is the Director of Food Services and The Ottawa Mission, and the owner of his non-profit service "Chef Ric's." We got to learn about his vulnerable childhood, and the story behind his organization.
